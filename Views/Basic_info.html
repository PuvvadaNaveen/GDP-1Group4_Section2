Physical Limitations

If you have any illness, injury, prosthesis, or physical limitation that might affect the comfort or fit of your costume, please discuss this privately with the costume designer. Some examples:

  • knee brace
  • scoliosis
  • asymmetry (one limb significantly longer or larger than the other)
  • arthritis/dexterity problems
  • need specific undergarments for comfort or modesty
  • prefer compression tights
  • use of prosthesis
  • use of mobility aid
  • unable to sweat/regulate body temperature
  • need sweatguards
  • specific shoe style needed

Please only disclose as much information as you are comfortable with and is relevant to the fit and comfort of your costume. This will insure that the costume designer can make you look your best!
